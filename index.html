Ylia Callan

Author • Researcher • Musician

Ylia Callan is an Australian folk musician, independent writer, and researcher whose work bridges physics, philosophy, and consciousness. Based in Uki, NSW, he blends fingerstyle and slide guitar on 6- and 12-string acoustics, creating music shaped by natural landscapes and timeless storytelling traditions.

As a writer and researcher, Callan explores how cosmic processes—light, structure, gravity, and information—shape life, culture, and the evolution of intelligence. His books combine scientific curiosity with accessible, reflective narrative, offering readers a pathway to rethink the relationship between the universe and human awareness.
